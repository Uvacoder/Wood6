Welcome to B&M Wood Designs

We make creative cutting boards, coasters, bottle openers, tables and pretty much anything else you can make out of wood.

TABLES

We make stunning tables from a wide array of wood. We have used typical hard wood speices, as well as reclaimed barn wood to create beautiful dining tables that will last. No particle board crap here, we only use the best woods we can get our hands on. As you can see, we have even used colored epoxy to create a stream effect on a live edge. If you can dream it, we can do it.

CUTTING BOARDS

Cutting boards is how it all began. Probably the best gift you could buy for someone who loves to be in the kitchen. Cut up some veggies, or maybe use it as a platter to serve some meat and cheese. Not only do they serve their designed purpose, but the look great doing it. Let us know if you have something specific in mind and we can probably make it happen.

MISCELANEOUS WOODEN ITEMS

We've done all kinds of stuff from corn hole boards to coasters to bottle openers to growth charts to track the growth of your children. We've even made custom dry bars. We pride ourselves with being open to just about anything you can come up with. If we can't do it, we'll let you know. But we will be sure to explore all avenues before calling it quits on your idea. If you're unsure, just click the special orders link at the top of the page and fill out the form. We will talk it over and get back to you as soon as we have a plan.
